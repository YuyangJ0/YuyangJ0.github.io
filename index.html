Research Focus: Human-Centered AI Development

  • From AI: Aligning LLM/VLLM for Responsible Human Use
    • Evaluate risks and biases in state-of-the-art LLM/VLLM systems to support responsible deployment.
    • Construct and curate high-quality, well-structured datasets (embedded with human reasoning) for auditing LLM/VLLM performance.
    • Post-train LLM/VLLM behavior to ensure alignment, particularly in high-stakes domains such as healthcare.
  • From Human: Empowering Humans for Better Interaction with LLMs
    • Study the bi-directional and temporal impact between humans and LLM-powered systems.
    • Build reliable human-AI interactive applications that safely serve target users and enhance human performance.

Collaborative Disagreement Resolution Outperforms Debate

By Yuyang Jiang*, Chacha Chen*, Shi Feng, Chenhao Tan ¬∑ Dec 20, 2025

Debate is the common approach in oversight. Typically it is framed as two models defending their own positions. However, that can be counterproductive, especially in cases with reasoning paths. We could get two models to collaborate and resolve the disagreement instead.

One possible failure mode is that the models are too sycophant. So one hypothesis is that highly sycophantic models will fail at disagreement resolution.
